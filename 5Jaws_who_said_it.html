JAWS — Who Said It?
"You're gonna need a bigger boat."
Challenge: One of Amity Island's residents made the most famous line in JAWS. Figure out who said it.
Amity Police • Marine Division
Task:
  1. Look at the quotes dictionary.
  2. Identify the character who said the line supplied to the function.
  3. Return their name exactly as shown in the dictionary.
  4. If correct, you'll see the passphrase.

Example:

quotes = {
    "Quint": "I'll never put on a life jacket again.",
    "Hooper": "This was no boating accident!"
}
who_said = "I'll never put on a life jacket again."
