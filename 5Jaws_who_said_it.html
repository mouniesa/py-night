Challenge: One of Amity Island's residents made the most famous line in JAWS: "You're gonna need a bigger boat." Figure out who said it.

Task:

  1. Look at the quotes dictionary.
  2. Identify the character who said the line supplied to the function.
  3. Run your script and return their name exactly as shown in the dictionary.
  4. If correct, you'll see the passphrase. Submit that passphrase.

Example: 
quotes = {
    "Quint": "I'll never put on a life jacket again.",
    "Hooper": "This was no boating accident!"
}
who_said = "I'll never put on a life jacket again."
